Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

Common questions about our YouTube transcript tool

### What is a YouTube transcript?

A YouTube transcript is a text version of the spoken content in a video. It captures all dialogue, narration, and sometimes even background audio in written form. Our tool extracts these transcripts directly from YouTube, whether they're automatically generated by YouTube or manually added by the video creator. This makes video content accessible in text format for reading, searching, or repurposing.

### How do I use the YouTube transcript tool?

Using our tool is simple: just paste the YouTube video URL into the input field at the top of the page and click the "Transcribe Video" button. Our system will process the request and display the complete transcript with timestamps. You can then read it alongside the video, copy the entire transcript with one click, or use our AI features to summarize or analyze the content.

### Can I generate transcripts for any YouTube video?

Our tool can generate transcripts for any public YouTube video that has captions available, whether they were added by the creator or automatically generated by YouTube. Some videos may not have transcripts available if the creator has disabled captions or if YouTube hasn't processed automatic captions for that video. In rare cases where automatic captions are unavailable, our advanced AI processing may still be able to generate a transcript.

### Can I translate YouTube transcripts to other languages?

Yes, our tool supports translation of transcripts into over 100 different languages. After generating a transcript, you can select your desired language from the dropdown menu to translate the entire content instantly. This feature is perfect for accessing content in your preferred language or reaching international audiences with your own video content.

### How accurate are the generated transcripts?

The accuracy of transcripts depends on several factors. Transcripts manually added by video creators are typically very accurate. YouTube's auto-generated captions have improved significantly and usually achieve 85-95% accuracy for clear audio in English. Our AI enhancement process can further improve accuracy by correcting common errors based on context. However, videos with poor audio quality, heavy accents, technical jargon, or background noise may have reduced accuracy.

### What can I do with the transcript once I have it?

The possibilities are endless! You can use transcripts for: creating blog posts from video content; generating study notes from educational videos; improving SEO by adding text content to your video descriptions; making your content accessible to hearing-impaired viewers; creating translated versions of your content; quickly searching for specific information in long videos; feeding into AI tools for summaries or content repurposing; and creating quotes or highlights for social media content.

### Is there a limit to how many transcripts I can generate?

For free users, we provide a generous allocation of transcript generations per day, which is sufficient for most casual users. For power users, content creators, researchers, or businesses who need to process larger volumes of videos, our premium plans offer higher or unlimited quotas to suit your specific needs.
